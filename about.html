About SwiftBus Botswana

Modern, reliable, and affordable bus travel across Botswana.

Our Story

SwiftBus Botswana was created to simplify long-distance travel by bringing bus booking online. We connect major cities such as Gaborone, Francistown, Maun, Kasane, and more.

Our goal is to make travel stress-free, affordable, and accessible to everyone in Botswana.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and efficient transport services through a modern digital booking system that saves time and improves convenience.

Our Vision

To become Botswana’s leading digital transport booking platform.
